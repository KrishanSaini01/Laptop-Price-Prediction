About Our Project

A Machine Learning based Laptop Price Prediction System that estimates laptop prices using hardware specifications.

Our Mission

LapPredict is designed to estimate laptop prices using Machine Learning. The system analyzes important hardware features such as Processor, RAM, GPU, Storage, Operating System, Display Resolution and Weight to predict an approximate market price.

The objective of this project is to help buyers, students and sellers quickly estimate laptop prices without manually comparing hundreds of products.
